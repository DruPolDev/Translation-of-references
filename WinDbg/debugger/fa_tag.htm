FA_TAG

The values of FA_TAG enumeration are tags that indicate the kind of information that is stored in failure analysis entry.

FA_TAG is another name for the DEBUG_FLR_PARAM_TYPE enumeration.

typedef DEBUG_FLR_PARAM_TYPE FA_TAG;
